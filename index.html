Christopher McKay is a Toronto-based director-of-photography who has shot subjects ranging from elephant births to ancient Viking ruins to children’s puppet shows for Disney and The Jim Henson Company. He has created work shown in broadcast formats all over North America and Europe including CBC, IFC, Playhouse Disney, Nickelodeon, National Geographic, TLC, and CTV. He has also shown at film festivals worldwide including the Toronto International Film Festival, The Annecy International Animation Film Festival, The Los Angeles International Short Film Festival, and the Ontario Gallery of Art.
