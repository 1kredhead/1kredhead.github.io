        Christopher McKay, DOP (416) 938-2523

        With over 25 years in the Canadian film and television industry, Christopher McKay is a Toronto-based director-of-photography who has shot subjects ranging from elephant births to ancient Viking ruins to children’s puppet shows for Disney and The Jim Henson Company. He has created work shown in broadcast formats all over North America and Europe including CBC, HBO, Playhouse Disney, Nickelodeon, National Geographic, TLC, and CTV. Corporate clients include McDonald's, Siemens, Apple TV, Mazda, Mitsubishi, and many others. He has also shown at film festivals worldwide including the Toronto International Film Festival, Hot Docs, The Annecy International Animation Film Festival, The Los Angeles International Short Film Festival, and the Art Gallery of Ontario.
